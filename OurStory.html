Our Journey in Wildlife Conservation

Founded in 2010, AnimalSave India has grown from a small grassroots initiative to a nationally recognized conservation organization. Our work spans across 15 states, protecting endangered species and their habitats through scientific research, anti-poaching efforts, and community education programs.

Our Documentaries

We've created powerful documentaries to showcase the beauty of India's wildlife and the urgent need for conservation. These films highlight our work, the challenges we face, and the inspiring stories of human-animal coexistence.

Our Mission in India

We work to protect India's rich biodiversity through science-based conservation strategies that balance ecological needs with sustainable human development. Our approach combines cutting-edge research with traditional ecological knowledge to create solutions that benefit both wildlife and local communities.

From the snow leopards of the Himalayas to the marine life of the Andaman Islands, we're committed to preserving India's natural heritage for future generations through habitat protection, species recovery programs, and environmental education.
